ABOUT THE GAME

UNIRETRO is a love letter to campus life. We've built an intricate collision system that lets you navigate the bustling squares and quiet parks of the university.

Explore every nook and cranny. From the massive library tower to the hidden shortcuts through the forest zones, your campus is now your playground.
